Pigeon Express

Carrier Pigeon Service for Letters

How does it work?
Carrier pigeons are trained by gradually increasing the distance between their home loft and a desired destination. The process involves repeated trips, creating a reliable homing instinct in the pigeons. Initial short flights are gradually extended, and the pigeons learn to return home from increasing distances. Training often includes positive reinforcement such as food rewards upon successful returns. Express Pigeons have an important role for our community and are treated with dignity and respect. Each of our pigeons enjoy a vacation twice a year to our roosting tavern where they enjoy six weeks of rest, ample food and community. Upon their third year, they return and retire to this facility permanently.
